Trust & Safety

Mentor Verification

Mentors submit proof such as offer letters, ID cards, or degree certificates. All documents are manually reviewed before approval.

Session Integrity

Sessions are expected to be respectful and professional. Any misuse is investigated.

Quality Monitoring

We collect feedback after sessions. Mentors with consistently poor feedback are removed.

Refund & Replacement Policy

If a mentor does not join or quality issues are verified, we offer replacements or refunds.

User Safety

Harassment, impersonation, or misleading claims lead to immediate account suspension.
