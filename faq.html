Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking a guidance session.

What is Project Guide?

Project Guide is a 1:1 guidance platform where students and professionals can speak directly with verified mentors for college counselling, career guidance, and interview preparation.

Who are the mentors?

Mentors are current or former students and professionals who have cleared admissions, built careers, or cracked interviews at reputed companies and colleges.

How are mentors verified?

Every mentor submits college or employment proof (offer letter, ID, degree). Our team manually verifies these documents before activating the mentor.

Can mentors give wrong or misleading advice?

We strictly monitor mentor feedback and session outcomes. Mentors with poor ratings or misleading guidance are removed from the platform.

Can I choose my mentor?

At the moment, we assign the most suitable available mentor based on your requirement to ensure faster bookings and quality guidance.

What is the duration of a session?

You can choose between 30-minute, 1-hour sessions, or extended interview preparation plans depending on your needs.

How are sessions conducted?

Sessions are conducted via video or audio call (Google Meet / Zoom). Session details are shared after booking.

What payment methods are supported?

Currently, we support UPI-based payments. More payment options will be introduced soon.

Is there a refund policy?

If a mentor does not join or a genuine issue is reported within 24 hours, we provide a replacement session or refund as applicable.

Are sessions recorded?

Sessions are not recorded by default. Recording requires consent from both mentor and mentee.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your personal information and session details are kept confidential and are never shared without permission.

How can I contact support?

You can reach us via the Contact page or email us at support@projectguide.in.
